# Weekly Routine

The default shape of my week when nothing special is going on.

## Mornings
- 6:30 wake, water + sunlight.
- Morning brief from my assistant over breakfast.
- Gym or sprints depending on the [[football-training-plan]].

## Weekdays
- School 8am–3pm.
- Football practice Tuesday and Thursday evenings, 7–9pm.
- Content batch (clip editing) Monday and Wednesday nights.

## Weekends
- Saturday: game day in season.
- Sunday: recovery, meal prep, and a weekly review of notes + goals.

Fixed commitments go on the calendar; everything else is flexible. #routine
