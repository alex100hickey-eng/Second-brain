# Faceless YouTube Notes

Research on building a faceless channel that compounds.

## Channel Thesis
Pick one narrow niche (e.g. "NFL film breakdowns") and never drift. The algorithm
rewards channels it can predict. Breadth kills faceless channels.

## Production System
- Script → AI voiceover → stock/gameplay B-roll → captions.
- Batch record 5 videos at once to beat the activation-energy problem.
- Thumbnail first, then title, then script. If you can't make a clickable thumbnail, skip the idea.

## Monetization Stack
- AdSense once at 1k subs / 4k watch hours.
- Affiliate links in description.
- Eventually a low-ticket digital product.
