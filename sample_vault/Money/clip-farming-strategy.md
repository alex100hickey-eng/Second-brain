# Clip Farming Strategy

The plan for making money with short-form clips of streamers and podcasts.

## Core Loop
1. Pull the 3–5 highest-energy moments from a long stream/podcast.
2. Cut vertical, add punchy captions, hook in the first 1.5 seconds.
3. Post across TikTok, Reels, and Shorts — same clip, platform-native captions.
4. Funnel views to a faceless channel with affiliate + creator-fund monetization.

## What Actually Gets Views
- Conflict, hot takes, and "wait for it" payoffs outperform everything.
- The hook is 80% of the result. If the first frame doesn't stop the scroll, nothing else matters.
- Consistency > perfection. 3 posts/day beats 1 polished post.

## Revenue Targets
- Month 1: break even on editing time.
- Month 3: $1,000/mo across creator funds + one affiliate deal.
