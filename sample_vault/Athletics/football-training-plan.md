# Football Training Plan

My in-season plan to stay explosive without burning out before games.

## Weekly Schedule
- **Monday** — Lower body lift (squats, RDLs, hip thrusts) + film review
- **Tuesday** — Team practice (7–9pm)
- **Wednesday** — Upper body lift + 20 min mobility
- **Thursday** — Team practice (7–9pm)
- **Friday** — Walkthrough + light sprints
- **Saturday** — Game day
- **Sunday** — Recovery: sauna, stretching, cold plunge

## Speed Work
Focus on the first 10 yards. Acceleration beats top speed for a running back.
Do 6x10yd sprints with full recovery on Friday only. See also [[sprint-mechanics]].

## Notes
Protein target is 1g per lb bodyweight. Sleep is the #1 recovery lever — 8+ hours.
