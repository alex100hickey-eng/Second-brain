# Sprint Mechanics

Cues that actually moved my 40 time down.

## Acceleration Phase
- Push the ground away, don't reach for it — big shin angle out of the stance.
- Arms drive back hard; the front swing takes care of itself.
- Stay low for the first 6–8 steps, rise gradually. Popping up early kills acceleration.

## Top Speed
- Front-side mechanics: knee up, toe up, strike down under the hip.
- Relax the face and hands — tension slows you down.
