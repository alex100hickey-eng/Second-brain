# Spanish Study Notes

Working toward conversational Spanish by the end of the year.

## Daily Routine
- 15 min Anki (spaced repetition) every morning.
- One podcast episode (Dreaming Spanish, intermediate) on the walk to class.
- Journal three sentences at night about my day, in Spanish.

## Grammar I Keep Forgetting
- **Ser vs Estar** — ser for permanent/identity, estar for state/location.
- Preterite = completed actions; imperfect = ongoing/background past.
- Subjunctive triggers: wishes, doubts, emotions, impersonal expressions.

## Vocabulary Themes
Food, directions, and small talk first — the stuff you actually use traveling.
Immersion beats memorization. Comprehensible input is the whole game.
