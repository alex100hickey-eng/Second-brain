The Ritual

Every chair at Ironside runs the same house sequence — perfected over eleven years, never rushed, never skipped. This isn't a haircut. It's forty-five minutes where nothing else gets in.

01 — The Hot Towel

Steamed linen straight from the warmer, laid over the face to open the pores and drop your shoulders before a blade ever touches you.

02 — The Straight Razor

A true edge, stropped in front of you, worked against the grain for a shave that lasts three days, not three hours.

03 — The Pour

A short glass of single malt, poured while your neck is finished with the trimmer. House standard is a ten-year Speyside — ask your barber what's open.
