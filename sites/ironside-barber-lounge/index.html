Questions Before You Sit Down

Our master barbers book out 1–2 weeks on average. Evening slots Thursday–Saturday go first. Book online or call the lounge directly — we hold same-day cancellations for members.
We keep a limited walk-in window Tuesday–Friday, 11am–1pm, subject to chair availability. Weekends are reservation-only. Text the front desk before you head over and we'll confirm a slot.
The Ironside Standing Chair membership includes one signature cut monthly, priority booking ahead of the public calendar, 15% off tonics and add-ons, and a reserved slot with your barber of record. Details and pricing are on the Services page.
We ask for 24 hours' notice. Cancellations inside that window, or no-shows, are charged 50% of the service price — your barber holds that time exclusively for you.
