Getting Here

Street parking downtown is a gamble. Plan around it and the visit starts calm instead of rushed.

Address

408 S. Spring St., Suite B (street level entrance, shop is downstairs). Downtown Los Angeles, CA 90013 — Historic Core, across from the Grand Central Market breezeway.

Parking & Valet

Valet available Thursday–Saturday, 3 PM to close, $12 flat. Self-park at the Pershing Square garage (2 blocks north) runs about $8/hr after validation from us — bring your ticket to the desk.

Transit

Pershing Square Metro station (Red/Purple Line) is a four-minute walk. DASH Route D stops directly outside. If you're biking in, we've got secure rack space just inside the entrance.

Inside a Visit

From the door to the mirror, roughly 55 minutes for a standard cut. Longer for the full ritual services. Nobody's rushing you back out.

Check-In

Ring the bell, give your name. We'll take your coat, pour you something — coffee, water, or a finger of bourbon if it's past noon.

Consultation

Your barber sits with you first. Face shape, hair pattern, how you actually style it at home. Two minutes that save you a bad six weeks.

The Chair

Cut, shape, and finish — scissor-over-comb precision, straight razor detail at the neckline. Hot towel if you're getting a beard trim.

Finish & Walk-Out

A final check in the mirror, product recommendations if you want them, no pressure if you don't. Settle up at the front, book your next date on the way out.

Client Notes

House rules, kept simple. This is a shop built on respect for the chair and the man in it.

Arrival

Show up 10 minutes early for your first visit — there's intake paperwork and a quick consult. After that, arriving right on time is fine.

Cancellations

Give us 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule without charge. Inside that window, a 50% fee applies to hold the barber's time.

Phones

Take the call if you need to. We'd just ask you keep it brief — this is 55 minutes that's supposed to be yours.
