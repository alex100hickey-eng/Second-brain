Location & Parking

The Address

412 S. Spring Street, Suite 101, Los Angeles, CA 90013 — street level, glass door, brass handle. No sign out front except a small brushed-steel plate. You'll know it when you see it.

Validated Garage Parking

Pull into the Spring Arcade Garage on 5th Street. Bring your ticket to the front desk — we validate for two hours, plenty for any service on the menu.

Street Entrance Notes

Our entrance sits between a coffee roaster and a tailor shop. If the outer gate is down, ring the buzzer marked "Ironside" — someone will let you in within a minute.

Getting Ready: Your First Visit

Arrive Ten Minutes Early

Settle in, hang your jacket, take a bourbon or black coffee from the bar. Your barber will come find you when the chair's ready — no waiting room shuffle.

The Consultation

Every first visit opens with five minutes of real conversation — your hair's history, your week ahead, what's worked and what hasn't. We build the cut around your life, not a template.

What to Bring

Nothing but yourself. If you've got a reference photo or a specific ask, we welcome it — but most men leave that at the door and trust the chair.

After the Chair

We'll walk you through upkeep — what product, what interval, what to ask for next time. Return visits get faster and sharper as your barber learns your head.

Walk-In Policy

We hold two chairs open daily for walk-ins, first-come basis, Tuesday through Saturday. Sundays are walk-in only. No guarantees, but rarely a long wait before 11 AM.
