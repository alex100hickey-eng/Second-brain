## Pages
- `index.html` — A cinematic scroll journey from the DTLA street into the lounge chair, building desire before presenting proof, pricing, and the booking CTA.
- `services.html` — Full detailed menu of services and pricing tiers, including membership options, so clients know exactly what to book and what it costs.
- `visit.html` — Practical logistics page for finding, parking, and booking at the lounge, reinforcing the premium in-person experience.

## Design system
- **Aesthetic:** Think private members' club at midnight: charcoal and near-black surfaces, brushed brass linework, and a single warm metallic accent that catches light like a razor's edge. Photography-style placeholders lean into deep shadow and rim-lit detail rather than bright, even studio lighting — every section feels like a frame from a slow cinematic dolly shot.
- **Fonts:** Marcellus (headings) / Jost (body) — loaded via Google Fonts.
- **Palette:** bg: #0e0d0c, surface: #1a1815, text: #ece7df, muted: #a39a8c, primary: #b6863f, accent: #c9a24b, border: #2f2a24, on_primary: #12100d
- All tokens live as CSS variables in `styles.css`.

## Structure
- `*.html` — the pages
- `styles.css` — the shared, hand-tuned stylesheet (single source of design truth)
- `main.js` — progressive-enhancement script (mobile nav, scroll-reveal animations, FAQ accordion, sticky-nav shadow)
- `serve.sh` — local preview server


## Cinematic intro
The home page opens with a scroll-scrubbed cinematic intro (6 scenes) that pins full-screen
and "travels in" as you scroll, then releases into the content. Scenes:

1. **THE CITY NEVER STOPS** — ideal footage: Wide dusk shot of downtown LA streets, long exposure traffic trails, steam rising from a grate, a single unmarked brass door glowing warm amid the cool city haze
2. **NO SIGN NEEDED** — ideal footage: Slow dolly-in toward a recessed charcoal storefront, brushed brass door handle catching a rim light, faint reflection of city lights on black granite
3. **THE DOOR OPENS** — ideal footage: POV push through the brass door, exterior street light spilling into a dark foyer, silhouette crossing the threshold into shadow
4. **A ROOM THAT WAITS** — ideal footage: Interior tracking shot across the lounge — black leather barber chairs, brass fixtures, a barber stropping a straight razor in soft amber light
5. **EVERY LINE EARNED** — ideal footage: Macro close-up of a straight razor gliding along a jawline, rim-lit steam from a hot towel, focused eyes of a barber mid-cut
6. **TAKE YOUR SEAT** — ideal footage: Slow reveal of an empty leather chair bathed in a single warm spotlight, brass instruments laid out, mirror reflecting dim gold light — inviting the visitor to sit

**These scenes use gradient placeholders — swap in real footage to make it truly cinematic.**
In `index.html`, find each `.cinema__bg` and set a real image/video still:
```html
<div class="cinema__bg" style="--scene-img: url('scene1.jpg');" ...></div>
```
Drop `scene1.jpg` (etc.) into this folder. Each scene already carries the ideal-shot description in an
HTML comment right above it. The scroll zoom, pinning, and headline timing all keep working unchanged.
