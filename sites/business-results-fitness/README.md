## Design system
- **Aesthetic:** Bright, gritty-athletic editorial: chalky off-white surfaces against near-black steel, punched through with a hot performance-orange accent like gym floor tape. Bold, stacked condensed headlines mimic gym signage and scoreboard type, paired with a clean humanist body face for readability. Photography-forward with strong diagonal cut dividers between sections to suggest motion and energy.
- **Fonts:** Anton (headings) / Work Sans (body) — loaded via Google Fonts.
- **Palette:** bg: #F7F4EE, surface: #FFFFFF, text: #191512, muted: #5B564F, primary: #141210, accent: #FF5A1F, border: #E4DFD4, on_primary: #F7F4EE
- All tokens live as CSS variables in `styles.css`.

## Structure
- `*.html` — the pages
