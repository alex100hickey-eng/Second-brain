1-on-1 Personal Training

The most direct route from where you are to where you want to be.

This is you and a coach, full stop. We start with a real assessment — movement, mobility, history, goals — then build a program around your body, your schedule, and your deadlines, not a generic template pulled off the internet.

Every session is programmed, watched, and adjusted in real time. If something hurts, we change it on the spot. If you're crushing it, we load you up. You'll never wonder if you're doing it right, because someone is standing right there making sure you are.

  • 60-minute sessions, 1x, 2x, or 3x per week
  • Custom program built around your goals and limitations
  • Direct text access to your coach between sessions
  • Progress check-ins every 4 weeks with measurable data

Small Group Coaching

All the coaching, none of the crowd. Groups of 4-6, max.

Small group mid-workout, coach cueing form

Small Group is where our members find their people. You're training alongside 3-5 others at a similar stage, with a coach running the session live — not on a screen, not on a loop. Everyone's weights and reps are scaled to them, but the energy in the room is shared.

It's the sweet spot for accountability: you've got teammates who notice when you skip a week, and a coach who's watching every rep. Most members tell us this is the first fitness routine they've actually kept for longer than three months.

  • 45-minute sessions, morning, midday, and evening times
  • Groups capped at 6 for real coaching attention
  • New programming block every 4 weeks — never stale
  • Unlimited or set-visit packages available

Nutrition & Accountability Coaching

Because training hard doesn't fix what happens at the dinner table.

Most people don't need another diet. They need someone checking in on Tuesdays. This program pairs you with a coach for weekly nutrition check-ins, habit-based goals, and honest conversations about what's actually happening outside the gym.

No point systems, no forbidden foods, no shakes to buy. We work with real food, your real schedule, and build habits that survive vacations, holidays, and bad weeks — because there will be bad weeks.

  • Weekly check-ins via app, text, or in person
  • Personalized habit and macro targets, adjusted monthly
  • Stacks with any training program or stands alone
  • Built for real life — travel, family dinners, birthdays

Semi-Private Strength Sessions

Serious strength training with a partner or two, same intensity as 1-on-1.

Two clients on adjacent racks under coach supervision

Semi-Private is for lifters who want the precision of personal training without going it alone. You and up to two others train together, on programs built individually but coached in the same window — think shared energy, individual bar weights.

It's a favorite with training partners, couples, and parent-and-teen pairs who want dedicated coaching without the full 1-on-1 price tag. Every rep still gets watched. Every program still gets built for the person doing it.

  • 50-minute sessions, groups of 2-3
  • Individualized programming per person, coached live
  • Ideal for strength, powerlifting, and return-from-injury goals
  • Bring your own partner or we'll match you
