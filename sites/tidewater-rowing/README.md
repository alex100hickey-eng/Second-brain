## Pages
- `index.html` — Welcome visitors, sell the feeling of dawn on the water and club camaraderie, and funnel them to programs/membership.
- `programs.html` — Detail the three program tracks so prospective members can self-select the right entry point.
- `membership.html` — Present pricing tiers clearly and encourage sign-up or trial.
- `contact.html` — Provide boathouse location, contact details, and a join/inquiry form.

## Design system
- **Aesthetic:** Think boathouse-at-dawn: soft fog-blue gradients over deep bay navy, warm sand and rope-tan accents, and clean nautical signage typography rather than kitschy anchors-and-rope clip art. Photography-led hero sections with a horizon-line motif and thin rule dividers evoke a dock plank or oar shaft, keeping the feel crisp, athletic, and unfussy.
- **Fonts:** Fraunces (headings) / Work Sans (body) — loaded via Google Fonts.
- **Palette:** bg: #F4EFE4, surface: #FFFFFF, text: #122B3A, muted: #5B7381, primary: #1C4A5E, accent: #C97D4B, border: #D8CDB8, on_primary: #F7F3E8
- All tokens live as CSS variables in `styles.css`.

## Structure
- `*.html` — the pages
- `styles.css` — the shared, hand-tuned stylesheet (single source of design truth)
- `main.js` — small progressive-enhancement script (mobile nav toggle)
- `serve.sh` — local preview server
