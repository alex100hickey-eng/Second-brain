How it works

Three steps, and Inkling does the tidying while you focus on the lecture.

✍️

1. Capture

Type, handwrite, or record — Inkling transcribes audio in real time and timestamps every line so you can jump straight back to the moment it was said.

🗂️

2. Organize

Notes are auto-sorted by course and date, key terms are highlighted, and related notes from prior weeks link themselves in — no folders to manage.

💡

3. Recall

Search by concept, not keyword. Ask "what did we cover on mitosis?" and Inkling surfaces every relevant note, even ones you forgot you took.
