Parking & Neighborhood Notes

Free parking is available in the lot directly behind the building, accessible from Birchwood Alley — enter past the mural of the oak tree. The lot holds about 20 cars and fills up on Friday and Saturday nights after 6:30 PM.

Overflow street parking is unrestricted after 6 PM along Birchwood Avenue and Second Street. If you're coming from downtown, the Maple Hollow Municipal Garage on Elm Street is a flat $3 evening rate and a five-minute walk.

Not driving? We're two blocks from the Birchwood bus line stop, and there's a bike rack by our front patio.

Private Events & Large Parties

Our back room, the Oak Room, seats up to 24 guests around a single long table beneath the exposed brick and string lights — ideal for rehearsal dinners, birthdays, and office gatherings that actually feel good to attend.

For parties of 9 or more in the main dining room, we ask for advance notice so we can plan the fire accordingly. We offer a family-style set menu for groups, with options for a full restaurant buyout on Mondays when we're normally closed.

To start planning, email us at events@emberandoak.example with your date, headcount, and occasion, and our events coordinator will follow up within two business days.
