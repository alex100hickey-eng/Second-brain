## Pages
- `index.html` — Make a strong first impression, communicate the brand story, and drive visitors to view the menu or reserve a table.
- `menu.html` — Present the full food and drink offering with prices in a scannable, appetite-driving format.
- `visit.html` — Give visitors everything needed to plan a visit: hours, location, contact, and directions.

## Design system
- **Aesthetic:** Feels like a converted brick warehouse dining room lit by the glow of an open hearth: deep charcoal walls, warm ember-orange accents flickering against them, and cream menu cards with letterpress-style headings. Typography pairs a characterful, slightly condensed serif display face with a clean humanist sans body, evoking a hand-painted chalkboard sign next to a polished printed menu.
- **Fonts:** Fraunces (headings) / Karla (body) — loaded via Google Fonts.
- **Palette:** bg: #231D1A, surface: #2E2622, text: #F3E9DA, muted: #C9B8A8, primary: #231D1A, accent: #E2622A, border: #4A3E35, on_primary: #F3E9DA
- All tokens live as CSS variables in `styles.css`.

## Structure
- `*.html` — the pages
- `styles.css` — the shared, hand-tuned stylesheet (single source of design truth)
- `main.js` — small progressive-enhancement script (mobile nav toggle)
- `serve.sh` — local preview server
