Chef's Note on Sourcing

Everything on this menu starts with a phone call, not a catalog. Our produce comes from Hollow Creek Farm, eleven miles up the road — we get a list of what's ready every Tuesday and build from there, which is why a dish might quietly change from one week to the next. Our flour is milled in-state; our mozzarella is made fresh three times a week by a supplier we've used since we opened. The oak and applewood in the oven come from a family sawmill that's been clearing storm-fallen trees for us since year one.

We're not chasing trends — we're chasing flavor, and flavor usually means fewer ingredients, handled better. If something on the menu looks simple, that's on purpose. Come hungry, and ask your server what came in this week. — Marco Sena, Executive Chef
